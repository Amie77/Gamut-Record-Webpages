GAMUT RECORD

An online independent record store that sells CD and vinyl albums from all time. We offer a variety of music selections for music lovers to discover hidden gems of the music industry.

Music Previews. Gamut Record is willing to help our customers with selecting albums. To ensure our customer gets the best shopping experience, we provide music previews for each album to help them get a beforehand savour of the music in advance of purchasing.

High-quality Products. All CDs and vinyl are carefully selected in terms of sound quality. We value good music, and want to present them to our customer in its best sound. You can only find the products with the best quality in our store.

Music Selection. We offer a wide range of music genres. You can find rock, jazz, blues, classical, pop, K-pop, electronic and country music in our online store. We embrace indie music, Gamut Record is definitely the haven for indie music lovers.
