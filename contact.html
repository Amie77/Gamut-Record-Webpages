TYPICAL SHIPPING

Standard or Free Shipping:
5-7 Businiess days after your order has shipped.

Priority Shipping:
3-4 Businiess days after your order has shipped.

Packages are typically delivered to your door. At times the carrier may deliver your package to a local office or store, to be picked up starting the next business day. You should always receive a notice when this happens, and you'll need to collect your package from the pickup location, or else it will be returned to Indigo’s distribution centre and your purchase refunded. The location of your collection point will be chosen by the carrier at the time of shipping will be in the general vicinity of your delivery address.

WRONG ITEM RECIEVED OR
ITEM DAMAGED

If you receive the wrong item or an item arrives damaged, please contact us and we’ll ship a replacement, or you can return that item and we’ll refund you.
If you accidentally receive something you didn't order and are charged for it, return the item for a full refund.

ITEM MISSING

We ship items in your order as they become available, so some multi-item orders will arrive in more than one package. If something appears to be missing:

1. Compare your shipping confirmation email(s) with your packing slip(s).
2. View your Order History in the Account Centre, or if you made your purchase as a guest, check the Order Status.
Once you've checked, contact us with your order number, the ISBN or SKU, and the title or name of any missing items.If we replace a missing item, product availability and delivery time may differ from that of the original purchase.

NO TRACKING INFO

If there is no tracking information, or you can't locate your package, please contact us within 30 days of receiving your shipping confirmation email.

INCORRECT ADDRESS

We ship to any Canadian or US address, and to a number of International Destinations, including to Post Office Boxes in North America. If you realise you entered a wrong or incorrect address after placing your order, please get in touch as soon as you can, with your order number and tracking information handy. You have 30 days from ship date to report a lost order.

DELIVERY STATUS

When your order ships, you'll receive an email notification with a tracking number and link to track your delivery. Items typically ship by next business day, but this will depend on item availability, shipping destination, and time of year.
Note: It can take up to 24 hours to see tracking information. Tracking is not available for Ship to Store orders, Gift Cards or plum membership cards. International orders will have tracking numbers but will not show status on our site.
